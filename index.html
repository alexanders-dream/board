About us

The Counsellors and Psychologist Board was established
on the 5th of August 2022. This momentous occasion was
made possible under the provisions of Section 3 of the
Counsellors and Psychologists Act No. 14 of 2014, which
laid the foundation for the creation of this regulatory
body.
