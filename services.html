Registration

Section 6 (d) and (c) of the Counselors and Psychologists Act (No. 14 of 2014) Revised Edition 2019 [2014] mandate the Board to (c) register and license counsellors and psychologists for the purposes of this Act upon payment of the prescribed fees.

Qualifications for registration

A person shall be eligible for registration under the Counselors and Psychologists Act (No. 14 of 2014) Revised Edition 2019 [2014] if the person—

  1. is, in the case of a counsellor, the holder of a diploma, degree in counselling from a recognized TIVET or University.
  2. is, in the case of a psychologist, the holder of a diploma, degree in psychology from a recognized university.
  3. has passed an examination prescribed by the Board; and
  4. satisfies the Board that he or she is a person of good moral character and a fit and proper person to be registered as stipulated in the Counselors and Psychologists Act (No. 14 of 2014) Revised Edition 2019 [2014].
  5. for the purpose of the first registration of counsellors and psychologists, a person qualified as a counsellor or psychologist shall be exempted from the requirements of section (c) above in accordance with the Counselors and Psychologists Act (No. 14 of 2014) Revised Edition 2019 [2014].
  6. a person who immediately before the first registration was engaged in private practice as a counsellor or psychologist shall be entitled to continue in such practice without a license, until the license is granted or finally refused, or the application is withdrawn.
